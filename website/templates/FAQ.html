Answers to frequently asked questions
How can I pay for my purchases in your store?

By placing an order on our website, you can pay online. Be careful! We do not accept payment by transfer to a card during the checkout process.

How soon will I receive my order?

It all depends on your location. All orders are shipped within a few days after payment of the order. *If you order an individual product (game sleeve), the delivery time may be extended.

How can I track my order?

We will send an order confirmation to your email with delivery information and tracking number. You can also track your order on our website.

How do I contact support?

If you have additional questions about the product, you can contact TG for more information.
